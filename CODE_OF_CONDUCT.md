# Code of Conduct

ModelMeld is a working community. Be excellent to each other; bring
your best work, give it room to breathe, and assume goodwill when
communication is ambiguous.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment of any kind
- Threats or incitement to harm
- Doxxing (publishing private information without consent)
- Spam or off-topic promotional content

## Enforcement

Reports go to `hello@modelmeld.ai`. Maintainers acknowledge reports
within 7 days. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or
reject contributions and to suspend or ban contributors at their
discretion when behavior violates these standards or undermines the
project's working environment. Repeated or severe violations result
in a permanent ban.

Reporters are kept confidential. We do not name reporters publicly.

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces (GitHub repos, Discussions,
official chat channels) and when you're representing ModelMeld in
public.
